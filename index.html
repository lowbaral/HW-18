About

I’m a lover of everything light and airy. Reading takes me to a place outside of reality and I love being able to imagine the words painted into a picture in my brain. My motto is "measure 5 times, cut once".

If I could sit and watch the wind blow along grassy fields or hear the ocean crash onto the shore everyday, I would consider that a life well-lived.

I’m much like a cat, if there is a sun spot in my home, I will lay in it.
